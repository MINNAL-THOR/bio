The Journey

Photography for Alan began as curiosity. Over time, it evolved into patience, observation, and understanding the rhythm of wildlife. Each frame reflects his connection with nature rather than just the subject.

Photographer Timeline

Alan started photography with simple curiosity about birds and forest life. Over time, he learned patience, timing, and storytelling. His school recognized his ability, giving him opportunities to document official events. Today, he continues building a personal wildlife archive that reflects emotion, silence, and nature’s raw beauty.

Achievements

Alan’s photography has been recognized in school-level platforms where his work stood out for composition and storytelling. He was selected for official school media photography responsibilities, marking his transition from hobbyist to a trusted visual storyteller. His ongoing wildlife portfolio is evolving into a strong creative identity.
